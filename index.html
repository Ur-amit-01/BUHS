Examination Scheme & Syllabus Coverage

Each theory paper shall be of 3 hours duration carrying 75 marks. The question paper pattern is as follows:

  • Section A: 10 compulsory short answer questions (2 marks each) → 20 marks
  • Section B: 5 out of 6 long answer questions (8 marks each) → 40 marks
  • Section C: 3 out of 4 analytical/case-based questions (5 marks each) → 15 marks

Syllabus coverage: Full syllabus as prescribed by Bihar University of Health Sciences, PCI norms. Topics include: BP101T (Tissues, skeletal system, body fluids), BP102T (Errors, volumetric analysis, acid-base titrations), BP103T (Dosage forms, prescriptions, posology), BP104T (Impurities, limit tests, medicinal gases), BP105T (Grammar, communication, presentation skills). Students are advised to refer to the official syllabus document available on the university website.

Minimum Passing Marks & Grading

As per University ordinance, a candidate must secure 50% marks (i.e., 38 out of 75) in each theory paper separately and 50% in practical examinations to pass the semester. Aggregate passing percentage required: 50% overall in the semester. Grading will be on a 10-point scale (CGPA). Students failing in one or more subjects will be eligible for supplementary examination as per university norms.
