Mandatory Registration for B. Pharmacy (2025–29 Batch) – Academic Year 2026-27

All students admitted to the Bachelor of Pharmacy program (Session 2025–29) are hereby directed to complete their compulsory university registration through their respective affiliated colleges. This is a one-time process as per University Ordinance 12.4.

Registration closes on: 14 April 2026 (11:59 PM)

Registration Procedure

  • Visit the administrative office of your affiliated college.
  • Submit self-attested copies of 10+2 marksheet, transfer certificate, migration certificate (if applicable), and category certificate.
  • Pay the registration fee of ₹500 via demand draft / online transfer as per college instructions.
  • Obtain the registration acknowledgment slip from the college.
  • Ensure your name is reflected as "REGISTERED" in the university portal by 20 April 2026.

Verification & Compliance

  • Cross-check your name, date of birth, and parent’s name as per 10+2 records.
  • Any discrepancy must be corrected before the deadline through the college.
  • Students already registered must collect a confirmation slip from the college.
  • Helpline: 0612-2546789 (10:00 AM – 5:00 PM)

Consequences of Non-Registration

  • Admission cancellation – The seat will be declared vacant and allotted to waitlisted candidates.
  • Examination ineligibility – No admit card will be issued for any semester examination.
  • Loss of student benefits – Hostel, library, scholarship, and ID card will be revoked.
  • Financial liability – Outstanding dues may be recovered as per university norms.
University Ordinance 12.4: “A candidate who fails to complete the registration formalities by the prescribed date shall forfeit his/her admission and shall not be permitted to appear in any university examination.”
